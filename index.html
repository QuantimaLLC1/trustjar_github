What is trustjar?

We are the anti-Ashley Madison: we’re here to keep people honest.

If you're in an exclusive relationship, declare it here. Connect your points of contact with theirs. Connect as many emails and phone numbers as you want. If someone else connects to ANY of those contacts, you’ll be notified and receive each other’s contact information.

If you're dating casually, maybe you want to be sure you’re not “the other woman” or man in someone’s exclusive relationship. By declaring your casual relationship here, you AND their “exclusive” partner will be notified along with each other’s contact information. Any other casual connections you (or they) make remains your own business.

Like trust itself, TrustJar works both ways, and the same rules apply to you as well as your partner. Otherwise, this connection remains completely private. No one's contact info is ever used for any other purpose. And your partner can’t refuse a relationship based on privacy concerns like they can with the rest of the social media circus.
