Tell us about your relationship.

What your partner doesn't say about your relationship speaks volumes. (more)

Start by creating a Trustjar relationship by entering the main email address or phone number for each of you below. Messages will be sent to confirm your respective identities, after which your relationship is established in a Trustjar. You can then add more contact channels to each side of the relationship if you wish.

What does this do for you? Let's say you want reassurances that you and your partner ARE exclusive, but you don't want to broadcast your relationship status to the world using something like Facebook. If your partner's contact channel is already in ANY other Trustjar relationship, they won't be allowed to accept your request. No other information about you or them or even whether they already belong to Trustjar is ever shared. All you'll ever know is if they accept your request, and once they accept it, you'll be notified if they leave the relationship. That's it.

Or maybe you're casually dating someone but don't want to be "the other person" in a love triangle. Enter your casual relationship here and if your partner is in an exclusive relationship, they won't be able to accept. And if they do and then down the road you meet someone else and want to be exclusive, you can end the casual relationship and your casual partner will only ever know that you ended the relationship with them. No other information will ever be divulged, and no one else will ever know about it. At least not from us.

So when you're in a Trustjar relationship, rest assured that your privacy and your relationship are both secure.
